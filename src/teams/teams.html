1.Tech Team

The Tech Team at GDSC is tasked with constructing websites, creating technical
documentation, and assisting with the development of technical
material for workshops and webinars.

2.Design Team

The Design Team at GDSC employs their creative abilities in utilizing various
graphic design techniques to conceptualize and craft visually appealing event
posters, as well as curating compelling content for social media platforms.

3.Android Dev Team

GDSC's Android Development team is responsible for designing and developing
mobile applications and projects, as well as facilitating workshops for
frameworks such as Flutter.

4.Social Team

The Content & Social Media Team at GDSC is responsible for producing and
disseminating content as well as overseeing the management of the
organization's social media platforms.

5.Community Team

Community Management Team is engaged in managing our active community of MVJCE
student developers and organising events and workshops.
